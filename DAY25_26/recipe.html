Barfi

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups khoya (mawa) or milk powder.
  2. 1 cup sugar
  3. 2 tbsp ghee
  4. Cardamom powder
  5. Nuts(optional, chopped)

Reciepe

  • Sugar syrup:Boil sugar + water till 1-string consistency.
  • Mix:Add khoya/milk powder + ghee, stir well.
  • Cook: Stir on low flame till mixture thickens and leaves sides of pan.
  • Flavor:Add cardamom and nuts.
  • Set:Spread mixture on greased plate, flatten, let cool.
  • Cut:Slice into squares/diamonds.
